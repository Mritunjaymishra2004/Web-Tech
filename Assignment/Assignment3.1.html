Form Observations

Google Sign-up Page Form
















Validation: Fields are required. Password and Confirm Password must match.

Amazon Login Page Form







Validation: Fields are required. Enter a valid email format. Password should be at least 6 characters.

Observations

  • Google Sign-up:
    • Form Fields: First Name, Last Name, Email/Phone, Password, Confirm Password
    • Purpose: Collects user personal information and account details
    • Validation: Fields are required. The Confirm Password field must match the Password field.
  • Amazon Login:
    • Form Fields: Email/Phone, Password
    • Purpose: Allows existing users to login to their account
    • Validation: Email field must follow a valid format, and Password must be at least 6 characters.
